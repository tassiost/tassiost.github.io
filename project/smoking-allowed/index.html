Smoking Allowed

We received the task to create a game in a group that would make people think about smoking and its development over the years.

The player gets to experience how prevalent smoking was in the past and then travel into the future after accomplishing a few tasks.

We used tunneling to help immerse the players more while simultaneously creating less nausea.
